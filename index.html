Shaurya 💛 Happy Birthday, Makhan 🧈 22 July
From: Shaurya  ·  To: Makhan 🧈  ·  Delivered: 22 July, your birthday

Kaira,

I built you a little computer. I know, very on-brand for me. But hear me out — I think it suits us. A bit ridiculous, a bit over-engineered, and somehow still kind of sweet.

You turn twenty-one today. I've had the privilege of knowing you for some of those years, and I want you to know that you are — genuinely, sincerely — one of the most wonderful people I've ever met. Not in a greeting-card way. In a "I think about something funny you said three months ago and still smile" way.

Thank you for putting up with me. Thank you for being soft when I need it and stubborn when I need that too. Thank you for being my Makhan — warm, golden, and somehow making everything better just by being there.

I hope twenty-one is everything you deserve. I'll be here for all of it.

Yours — Shaurya 💛
